How it works

Once you join, you and your crew get an invite to our Breeew kanban board, where you can team up with cpx.studio in real-time. From here, you can chat directly with cpx.studio, shooting over as many design and coding requests as you fancy.

I'll tackle each request step by step, and feel free to ask for as many tweaks as you need.

A typical example

Let's assume you're a startup in need of branding and a website, but your budget isn't sufficient to hire a full design and development agency. What we would do is start by creating several logo proposals in the initial days, then move on to corporate identity and branding. Once that's finalized, we proceed to website design and development. Throughout the process, we collaborate closely with you to ensure the end result aligns perfectly with your needs. This entire process typically spans about three weeks. In the final week of the month, we can craft social media posts, providing you with a comprehensive package for your startup. Should you require more advanced features like a webshop or a custom CRM system, we're open to discussing those options as well. Ultimately, our approach offers better value for your money compared to hiring an expensive team or attempting to handle everything in-house, especially if your team lacks certain skills.

The above mentioned example assumes you choose the Design + Dev subscription.
